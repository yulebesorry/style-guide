Baseline Reflection — Jun 2, 2026

Across all three assessments, the thread I keep noticing is autonomy — I score high on it in SCARF, creativity and curiosity show up as top VIA strengths, and visionary thinking lands as my core superpower. The shadow side of that is real though: overthinking and perfectionism both show up as the flip side of those strengths. What I want to track this course is whether I can channel the visionary energy without getting stuck in my own head. That's the practice.
